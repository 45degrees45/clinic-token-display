The Solution

Token appears on the TV. Automatically.

TokenQ is a digital queue system. The TV screen shows who's being seen now and who's next. Doctor calls the next token from their phone — without leaving the room.

1
🎫 Patient gets a token Receptionist issues a token number when the patient arrives. Or patient self check-in.
2
📱 Doctor taps "Call Next" on phone No need to walk out. Tap one button from inside the consulting room.
3
📺 Token shown on TV + voice announcement Patients in the waiting area see the number. English and Malayalam announcement plays automatically.
