Before the Meeting — Setup (30 seconds)

Step 1
Open the TV Display link on your laptop. Press F11 for fullscreen.
Step 2
Open the Admin link on your phone. Keep it in your hand.

The Demo — 2 Minutes

1
Show the waiting room screen
Point to the large token number and "Up Next" card on your laptop.
"This goes on any TV. Patients see their number. No more shouting names."
2
Press "Call Next Patient" on your phone
The TV updates instantly — new name, new number, audio announcement plays.
"You press this from your chair, from your consulting room, from anywhere in the clinic."
3
Show Urgent marking
Issue a new token, tick "Mark as Urgent" — that patient jumps to the front automatically.
"For elderly patients, chest pain, emergencies — one tap and they're next."
4
Show the patient view
Open the Patient link on a second phone. They see their token number and estimated wait time.
"Patients check their position from their own phone — no app, just a QR code at reception."
5
Show the language selector
Tick English + Dutch or English + Hindi in admin. Press Call Next — TV announces in both languages.
"Works in English, Dutch, Hindi, or Malayalam — whichever language your patients speak."
6
Close with setup
Any old TV, an HDMI cable, a cheap streaming stick.
"5 minutes to set up. No monthly fees — one-time setup."
